TRAVEL TIPS

Simple ways to get rid of the jet lag

Light is the strongest stimulus for realigning a person's sleep–wake schedule, and careful control of exposure to and avoidance of bright light to the eyes can speed adjustment to a new time zone. Travelling east by six to nine time zones causes the biggest problems, as it is desirable to avoid light in the mornings. Travelling west causes fewer problems than travelling east, and it is usually sufficient to seek exposure to light during the day and avoid it at night.
