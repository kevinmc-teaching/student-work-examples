Snowboard in Austria

As known as Europe snowboard capital, Austria has several resorts and mountains that attracts millions of tourists every winter. The most famous city, Tirol, has host big events and it is located in the middle of the alpis. Surrounded by gorgeous mountains, Tirol definetely is a place to consider visiting.

Another interesting fact about Austria is that all resorts offer free snowboard courses for their hosts. This saves you a lot of money because there are some places that cost you $250 daily for that kind of course. There are no age restriction to practice snowboard but it is required that the person doesnt have any knee issue or arthrose. Before booking your tickets, have a consult with your doctor and ask him if you are good to practice the sport.

Austria has also good cousine tradition and an active night life. After spending your day snowboarding, consider yourself to try Tafelspitz(boiled beef), the traditional hams or German sausages. After eating, a good suggestion is to go parties provided by the resorts. Some of them are even free for entrance.

New ski resort in Whistler

Whistler has one of the most famous ski resorts in the world. Placed around the Blackcome mountain, where 2010 Winter Olympics sliding sports took place, the resorts offer gondola passes to their hosts go to the top of the mountain. But, this year a brand new resort has been built to offer much more activities for the tourists.

PDM Resort, has 20% more infrastructure than its direct competitor Whistler Blackcome Resort. Also, the resort built an exclusive ski route that has difficult areas from beginners until professionals, including tunnels and caves that give the tourist an experience never seen before. Only PDM hosts can access this area and they dont need to pay any fee for it. Another new feature provided by the new resort is the Brazilian cousine restaurant. It has typical dishes from all Brazil`s regions and a variety of traditional drink, including caipirinha. The restaurant is opened for public but they need to reserve first.

PDM Resort is offering 20% discount for the first 100 guests that book their accomodation for this winter. Don't waste this exclusive opportunity and book your room on www.pdmresort.ca.
