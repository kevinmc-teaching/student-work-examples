Night in Tokyo

Tokyo is a beautiful city during the day, but at night it hides precious places. The city is safe, so it invites visitors from all over the world to explore its hidden treasures. Walk along the canals of Tokyo Bay and find beautiful buildings illuminated at night. Explore the neighbourhood that never sleeps: Roppongi. Find a night club to have some fun. Visit the man-made leisure island, Odaiba, and discover all the activities available to enjoy your night.

Food for your soul and your body

Explore the tastes and textures of Japanese food. From traditional Japanese restaurants with more than 10 courses to the cheapest fast food chains. In Tokyo you can find all kinds of food, worldwide cuisine, for all range of prices. Find cheap and expensive sushi, all sorts of Italian, Korean, Chinese, Indian restaurants. Usually well executed at affordable prices. It worths trying!
