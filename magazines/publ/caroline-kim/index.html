about us

There's little better than being indoors on a rainy day, your hands wrapped around a steaming cup of your favorite tea. The smell is intoxicating, and somehow both invigorating and relaxing at the same time. Maybe you have some music playing in the background and you're simply enjoying the scenery. Maybe you're rewatching an episode of your favorite tv show. Maybe you finally found the time to read that book you've had on your reading list for the past month and a half but couldn't get to because of life.

We've gathered a fantastic group of writers who can relate to this scenario on a spiritual level and have created Teahouse. We are a web magazine for and by tea enthusiasts around the world. We hope you enjoy reading what we've enjoyed writing.
